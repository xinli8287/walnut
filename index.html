FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about working with a Notion consultant.

What does a Notion consultant do?

A Notion consultant designs, builds, and optimizes Notion workspaces for teams and organizations. This includes workspace architecture, custom templates, relational databases, automations, integrations, team training, content migration, and ongoing advisory support.

How much does Notion consulting cost?

We offer hourly consultations for quick help, custom project-based quotes for larger builds, and monthly retainers for ongoing support. Every engagement starts with a free 20-minute discovery call to discuss your needs and recommend the best approach.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes! We offer a free 20-minute discovery call where we discuss your team's challenges, explore how Notion can help, and provide initial recommendations — with absolutely no obligation. It's the best way to see if we're a good fit.

Can you help migrate our data from other tools into Notion?

Absolutely. Our Content Migration service moves your knowledge and data from legacy tools like Google Docs, Confluence, Trello, Asana, Airtable, and spreadsheets into Notion — cleanly structured and ready to use from day one.

Do you work with teams outside of Melbourne?

Yes. While we're based in Melbourne, Australia, we work with teams globally. All our consulting, training, and workspace design services can be delivered entirely remotely via video calls and screen sharing.

What kind of Notion training do you provide?

We provide hands-on Notion training tailored to your team's workspace and workflows. This includes live interactive sessions, recorded walkthroughs, documentation of SOPs and playbooks, and change management support to ensure lasting adoption across your organization.
